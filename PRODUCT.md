# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Wishly is for people who keep personal or shared wishlists and want a calmer way to organize gifts, home items, setup upgrades, travel ideas, and purchase timing.

## Product Purpose

The product turns wishlists into curated collections with simple navigation, product cards, price tracking, activity updates, and a Pro flow for alerts and checkout.

## Brand Personality

Clear, practical, and restrained. The product should feel useful and mature, with enough visual polish to make lists feel intentional without becoming decorative.

## Anti-references

Avoid overly cute gifting apps, pastel-only palettes, frantic e-commerce urgency, cluttered deal dashboards, and decorative UI that gets in the way of browsing or tracking.

## Design Principles

- Keep the primary workflow obvious on a phone.
- Treat products and lists as visual collections, not dense spreadsheets.
- Use restrained contrast and simple surfaces so the app works for a broader audience.
- Make Pro features feel functional and valuable, not pushy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target clear contrast for light and dark themes, visible focus states where browser defaults apply, reduced visual clutter, and responsive layouts without horizontal overflow.
